# See's Candy

**Founded:** 1921
**Headquarters:** South San Francisco, California

## Overview

See's Candy is an iconic American confectioner with a particularly strong brand presence in California. Buffett purchased See's in 1972 for $25 million and it has since become one of his favorite examples of a business with a durable competitive moat.

## Why Buffett Loves See's

> "The See's decision was a turning point in our investment approach. It taught us that a great business could be worth much more than its tangible assets."
> — Charlie Munger

## Key Facts

- **Pricing Power:** See's has raised prices dramatically over the decades while maintaining loyal customers
- **Brand Value:** The See's brand is synonymous with quality in the Western US
- **Moat:** Switching costs and brand loyalty create a wide moat
