# Berkshire Hathaway

**Ticker:** BRK.A / BRK.B
**Founded:** 1839 (as a textile company)
**Headquarters:** Omaha, Nebraska

## Overview

Berkshire Hathaway started as a struggling textile company in Rhode Island. Buffett began accumulating shares in the 1960s and eventually transformed it into a diversified conglomerate and investment vehicle.

## Buffett's Involvement

> "I was fortunate in many ways. But nothing was as important to my investment success as the acquisition of Berkshire Hathaway."
> — 1996 Annual Letter

## Key Facts

- **Insurance Float:** Berkshire's insurance operations provide significant "float" for investments
- **Diversified Holdings:** From insurance to railroads to energy
- **50+ Subsidiaries:** Including GEICO, BNSF, Dairy Queen, and many others
