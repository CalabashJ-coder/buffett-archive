# Thanksgiving 2025

*Published: November 2025*

This letter marks one of Warren Buffett's final public communications before transitioning leadership to Greg Abel.

## Key Themes

- Gratitude for Berkshire Hathaway's journey over 60 years
- Reflections on the future of the company under new leadership
- The importance of American capitalism and long-term thinking
- Final thoughts on philanthropy and wealth distribution
