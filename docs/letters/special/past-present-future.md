# Berkshire Hathaway's Past, Present and Future

*Published: 2014*

This special letter was written to mark a significant moment in Berkshire Hathaway's history, reviewing where the company came from, where it is today, and where it is headed.

## Key Themes

- The evolution of Berkshire from a struggling textile company to a diversified conglomerate
- The role of insurance float in funding Berkshire's investments
- The importance of maintaining Berkshire's cultural identity under new leadership
- Reflections on the next generation of leadership under Greg Abel
