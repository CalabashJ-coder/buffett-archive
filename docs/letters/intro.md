# Shareholder Letters

This archive contains Warren Buffett's shareholder letters spanning over 70 years, from the early Partnership Letters (1956-1970) to the modern Berkshire Hathaway annual letters (1965-present).

## Structure

### Partnership Letters (1956-1970)
Buffett wrote 35 letters to partners in the Buffett Partnership Ltd., laying out his investment philosophy before acquiring Berkshire Hathaway.

### Berkshire Hathaway Letters (1965-2024)
Annual letters to Berkshire Hathaway shareholders, covering business performance, investment philosophy, and reflections on the economy.

### Special Letters
Occasional letters on specific topics, including the 2014 letter on Berkshire's past, present, and future, and the annual Thanksgiving messages.

## How to Navigate

- **By Year**: Browse letters chronologically from the sidebar
- **By Topic**: Use the Concepts section to find themes across multiple letters
- **By Company**: The Companies section tracks Buffett's analysis of specific businesses over time
