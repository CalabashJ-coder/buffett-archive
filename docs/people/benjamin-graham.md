# Benjamin Graham

**Born:** May 8, 1894
**Died:** September 21, 1976

## Background

Benjamin Graham was a professor at Columbia Business School and the author of two of the most influential investment books ever written: "Security Analysis" (1934, with David Dodd) and "The Intelligent Investor" (1949).

## Buffett's Teacher

Buffett studied under Graham at Columbia and worked at Graham's investment partnership before starting Berkshire. Graham's "Mr. Market" allegory and concept of intrinsic value became foundational to Buffett's investment approach.

## Key Concepts Introduced

- Intrinsic Value
- Margin of Safety
- Mr. Market
- Net-Net Working Capital

## Famous Quote

> "In the short run, the market is a voting machine. In the long run, it is a weighing machine."
> — Benjamin Graham
