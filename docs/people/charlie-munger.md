# Charlie Munger

**Role:** Vice Chairman, Berkshire Hathaway (1978-present)
**Born:** January 1, 1924
**Died:** November 28, 2023

## Background

Charlie Munger met Warren Buffett in 1959 and quickly became his closest friend and business partner. He served as Vice Chairman of Berkshire Hathaway from 1978 until his death in 2023.

## Investment Philosophy

Munger was known for his multidisciplinary approach to investing, drawing from psychology, economics, and philosophy.

> "Take a simple idea and take it seriously."
> — Charlie Munger

> "The first rule of investing is don't lose. The second rule is don't forget the first rule."
> — Charlie Munger

## Buffett on Munger

> "Charlie has been my partner and right-hand man for decades. His influence on Berkshire's culture and investment approach cannot be overstated."
> — Multiple annual letters
